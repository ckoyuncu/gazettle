Frequently asked

How do I play?

Gazettle gives you three fresh word puzzles every day:

  • Classic — guess the five-letter word of the day in six tries. Green = right letter, right spot; yellow = right letter, wrong spot; grey = not in the word.
  • Semantic — find the secret word by meaning. Each guess shows how close you are, warmer or colder.
  • Bullseye — arrange a chain of words so each connects to the next, ending on the target.

What is the daily streak?

Play the daily set and your streak grows each day you take part. Miss a day and it resets — your stats and best streak are kept on your device.

Are my stats saved if I change phones?

Your progress, stats, and streak are stored locally on your device, so they don't transfer automatically between devices.

Is Gazettle free? Does it collect my data?

Gazettle is free to play and keeps your data on your device — it collects no personal information and requires no account. See the full Privacy Policy.
